Forms - Memo Akten

http://www.memo.tv/forms Forms is an ongoing collaboration between visuals artists Memo Akten and Quayola, a series of studies on human motion, and its reverberations through space and time. It is inspired by the works of Eadweard Muybridge, Harold Edgerton, tienne-Jules Marey as well as similarly inspired modernist cubist works such as Marcel Duchamp's "Nude Descending a Staircase No.2.
